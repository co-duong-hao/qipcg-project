# Participant Instructions

Thank you for helping evaluate generated game levels.

You will rate several tile-grid levels. Each level was generated by one of
several algorithms, but the algorithm names are hidden. Please judge only what
you see.

For each level, rate:

- Playability: Does it look possible to navigate or complete?
- Style consistency: Does it look like it belongs to the stated game domain?
- Novelty: Does it look meaningfully different from the other examples while
  still making sense?
- Overall quality: Your overall impression of the level.

Use the full 1--5 scale. It is acceptable to give low scores to levels that
look broken, random, repetitive, or hard to understand.
