# Consent and Ethics Text

Use this text at the beginning of the survey. Adapt names/contact details before
sharing the form.

## Participant Consent

You are invited to take part in a small academic evaluation of generated game
levels. You will be asked to rate tile-grid levels on perceived playability,
style consistency, novelty, and overall quality.

Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time before submitting the
form. The survey does not ask for sensitive personal information. Responses
will be analyzed in aggregate for a research paper or project report. We will
not publish individual names or individual-level responses.

Estimated time: 15--25 minutes.

By continuing, you confirm that:

- you understand the task,
- your participation is voluntary,
- your responses may be used in aggregate for academic reporting,
- you do not expect compensation unless separately arranged by the research
  team.

## Data Handling

Collect only a participant code such as `P001`, not full names. If the team
needs to track who has responded, keep that contact list separate from the
rating CSV and do not include it in the analysis folder.

## Exclusion Rules

Before analysis, exclude a response only for pre-declared quality reasons:

- the participant did not complete all stimuli,
- ratings are outside the 1--5 scale,
- the same stimulus is rated more than once by the same participant,
- the response appears empty or mechanically repeated across all items.

Document exclusions in the final report.
